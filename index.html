From Cyberattack Victim to Cybersecurity Champion: Our Story

In the digital age, cybersecurity isn't just a luxury—it's a necessity. Yet, for many individuals and small businesses, robust protection remains out of reach, shrouded in complexity and exorbitant costs. Breaking Circuits, LLC, was born from the ashes of one founder's personal cyber nightmare, a harrowing experience that illuminated the desperate need for accessible and affordable cybersecurity solutions.

Our founder, a disabled entrepreneur, knows firsthand the devastating impact a cyberattack can inflict. It began innocuously enough: a seemingly harmless phishing email that slipped past the existing defenses. But soon, the insidious malware spread like wildfire, crippling critical systems and jeopardizing sensitive data. What followed was a grueling two-and-a-half-month ordeal, a relentless battle against faceless adversaries who sought to exploit vulnerabilities and wreak havoc.

With limited financial resources and no access to professional help, the founder was left to fend for themselves, facing immense challenges alone. Imagine the feeling of isolation, the weight of responsibility as you watch your digital life crumble around you. The founder was forced to dedicate up to 12 hours a day to studying, experimenting, and piecing together a defense against the relentless assault.

Through sheer perseverance, countless hours of research, and a relentless commitment to learning, the founder managed to repel the attackers. This victory, however, was bittersweet. The experience left an indelible mark, exposing the harsh realities of the cybersecurity landscape:

  • High Costs as Barriers: The high costs associated with professional cybersecurity services create significant barriers to entry, leaving many vulnerable and unprotected.
  • Lack of Accessible Support: Finding reliable and affordable help during a cyberattack is incredibly difficult, leaving victims feeling isolated and overwhelmed.
  • The Digital Divide: The digital divide exacerbates these challenges, as underserved communities lack the resources and expertise to defend themselves against increasingly sophisticated threats.

This personal crucible ignited a deep-seated passion to help others avoid the same fate. Thus, Breaking Circuits, LLC, was conceived as a beacon of hope, a company committed to democratizing cybersecurity and empowering individuals and communities to navigate the digital world with confidence.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is simple: to provide transparent, affordable, and accessible cybersecurity solutions using open-source software. We focus on empowering underserved communities and fostering a diverse and inclusive cybersecurity workforce. We envision a world where everyone, regardless of their financial means or technical expertise, has access to robust cybersecurity services. We strive to bridge the digital divide and promote diversity within the cybersecurity field, creating a more equitable and secure digital future for all.

Our Commitment to Open Source

Breaking Circuits exclusively uses open-source software, promoting transparency and affordability. Open-source solutions provide greater flexibility, community support, and the ability to customize security measures to meet specific needs. By leveraging these tools, we can keep costs low and deliver effective solutions tailored to our clients' unique challenges.

We operate on a volunteer-based workforce model supervised by experienced professionals. This innovative approach not only allows us to offer cost-effective services but also creates a pipeline for qualified cybersecurity professionals. We provide training, mentorship, and real-world experience to our volunteers, fostering a diverse and skilled workforce ready to tackle the cybersecurity challenges of tomorrow.
